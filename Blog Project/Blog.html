  • Resin of farming

    We must use our daily resin wisely and not waste it on unrequired stuffs. Priority order for where to spend our resin should be:

    1. Character level
    2. Weapon level
    3. Talent level
    4. Artifacts



    Investing in artifacts is a waste of mora and resin. Farm for artifacts only when your character is weak and you desperately need proper artifacts.
